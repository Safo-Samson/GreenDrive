This repository is about Green Drive, the aim of which is to serve as a platform to streamline the vehicle conversion process from fuel to hybrid electric. It intends to provide a software application that offers comprehensive information and resources to individuals and organisations who are converting their vehicles to hybrid electric vehicles. Users will be able to easily grasp the steps, prices, cost tracking, news and guides, and advantages of converting their automobile to a hybrid electric vehicle thanks to the application which will serve as a one-stop shop for anything electric vehicle conversion-related.
